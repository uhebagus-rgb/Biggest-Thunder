The journey so far

Founded by a team of travel industry veterans and tech innovators, Biggest Thunder emerged from years of listening to parents' frustrations. We saw that while travel technology was advancing, it wasn't keeping up with the real needs of families.

Our flagship Thunder device is the result of countless conversations, prototypes, and real-world testing with parents just like you. It's not just a gadget — it's a travel companion that gives you confidence, connection, and peace of mind.

Today, we're proud to support families around the world. And we're just getting started.
